# Starter Packs: Industry-Focused Skill Collections

Welcome to Starter Packs — curated collections of skills designed for specific industries and business models. Each pack gives you a proven foundation to launch marketing operations quickly, with a recommended sequence for adoption.

## What Are Starter Packs?

Starter Packs are themed collections of 8-20 skills grouped by industry and use case. Instead of choosing from 156 skills, you get a focused roadmap with:

- **Clear sequencing** — which skills to learn first, second, etc.
- **Industry context** — why each skill matters for your business
- **Expected outcomes** — what you'll deliver using this pack
- **Quick start guidance** — which data to gather and which skill to try first

**They're not prescriptive.** Customize by adding other skills, skipping ones that don't fit, and reordering based on your priorities.

## 1. SaaS B2B Starter Pack

**For:** SaaS companies, B2B software vendors, PLG platforms, enterprise tools

**Why This Pack:** SaaS marketing is driven by product-led growth metrics, lead quality, and multi-touch attribution. You need to understand your ICP, generate qualified leads, nurture them through long sales cycles, and measure what actually moves the needle. This pack builds the foundation for data-driven, efficient SaaS growth.

### Skills (In Recommended Order)

1. **[Customer Persona Builder](skills/growth/customer-persona-builder.md)** → Start here. Define your ideal customer profile from surveys, interviews, or analytics. All downstream strategy depends on understanding who you're targeting.

2. **[Lead Scoring Model Builder](skills/crm/lead-scoring-model-builder.md)** → Qualify leads based on firmographic and behavioral signals. Prevents sales team burnout on bad leads.

3. **[Content Brief Generator](skills/seo/content-brief-generator.md)** → Identify high-intent keywords your ICP is searching for. Target problems they're trying to solve.

4. **[Keyword Cluster Analyzer](skills/seo/keyword-cluster-analyzer.md)** → Organize keywords into topic clusters. Map clusters to your product features and GTM messaging.

5. **[Email Sequence Builder](skills/email/email-sequence-builder.md)** → Design nurture sequences for different lead stages. Convert MQLs to SQLs through targeted education.

6. **[UTM Tracking Manager](skills/martech-ops/utm-tracking-manager.md)** → Standardize campaign tracking so you can see which channels, campaigns, and tactics actually drive conversions.

7. **[Marketing ROI Calculator](skills/analytics/marketing-roi-calculator.md)** → Measure ROI, ROAS, and CAC across paid and organic channels. Make budget allocation decisions based on data.

8. **[Landing Page Optimizer](skills/cro/landing-page-optimizer.md)** → Audit landing pages for conversion friction. High-intent traffic only converts if landing pages are crisp.

9. **[Account Research Synthesizer](skills/b2b/account-research-synthesizer.md)** → For ABM plays on enterprise accounts. Synthesize all intel into a brief your team can act on.

10. **[Buying Committee Mapper](skills/b2b/buying-committee-mapper.md)** → Map decision-makers and their priorities in target accounts. Identify the coach, champion, and economic buyer.

11. **[Ad Copy Generator](skills/ads/ad-copy-generator.md)** → Write high-converting paid search and social ads. Test messaging angles quickly.

12. **[Attribution Model Builder](skills/analytics/attribution-model-builder.md)** → Move beyond last-click attribution. Understand which touchpoints actually move deals forward.

13. **[Voice of Customer Miner](skills/insights/voice-of-customer-miner.md)** → Mine Reddit, forums, and review sites for authentic customer language. Inform messaging and positioning.

14. **[Campaign Angle Generator](skills/insights/campaign-angle-generator.md)** → Generate campaign angles using proven persuasion frameworks. Create multiple messaging hooks from one insight.

15. **[Growth Experiment Designer](skills/growth/growth-experiment-designer.md)** → Design hypothesis-driven growth tests. Move from "let's try this" to "let's test that."

### Expected Outcomes

- **Lead quality increases by 40-60%** through better targeting and lead scoring
- **Sales cycles shorten by 2-3 weeks** because leads are better-qualified and more engaged
- **CAC falls by 25-35%** as you optimize landing pages and trim underperforming channels
- **Team alignment improves** because everyone speaks the same language about ICP, lead stages, and messaging
- **Attribution clarity** — you know which campaigns and channels are actually driving revenue

### Quick Start

1. **Week 1:** Run Customer Persona Builder on your last 10 customers. Spend 30 mins talking to 1-2 of them. Export the persona to your sales team.
2. **Week 2:** Export your CRM leads from the last 6 months. Run Lead Scoring Model Builder. Share thresholds with sales for triage.
3. **Week 3:** Export your top 20 keywords from Google Ads / organic. Cluster them with Keyword Cluster Analyzer. Plan one pillar page.
4. **Week 4:** Build a 5-email nurture sequence with Email Sequence Builder. Test it on your own list first.

Start there. Add 1 skill per week from the list above.

**Why This Pack:** E-commerce success is conversion-driven, data-intensive, and seasonal. You need to turn browsers into buyers, optimize product pages and checkout flows, retain customers after purchase, and maximize customer lifetime value. 5. **[Email Sequence Builder](skills/email/email-sequence-builder.md)** → Design post-purchase email sequences (thank you, feedback, upsell, cross-sell, loyalty).

6. **[Welcome Series Creator](skills/email/welcome-series-creator.md)** → Activate new subscribers with a 3-5 email series. Drive first purchase and build habit.

7. **[Marketing ROI Calculator](skills/analytics/marketing-roi-calculator.md)** → Calculate LTV, CAC, and ROAS by channel. Find your highest-ROI traffic sources.

8. **[UTM Tracking Manager](skills/martech-ops/utm-tracking-manager.md)** → Track every paid and organic channel with consistent naming. Know which ads, influencers, and emails drive revenue.

9. **[Retargeting Campaign Builder](skills/ads/retargeting-campaign-builder.md)** → Capture abandoners with pixel-based retargeting. Recover lost sales through Facebook / Google remarketing.

10. **[Form Optimization Toolkit](skills/cro/form-optimization-toolkit.md)** → Optimize email capture forms. Reduce fields, increase conversions, segment by intent.

11. **[Social Content Calendar](skills/social/social-content-calendar.md)** → Plan Instagram, TikTok, and Pinterest content. Tease products, share UGC, drive traffic.

12. **[Email List Segmentation](skills/email/email-list-segmentation.md)** → Segment customers by purchase history, RFM, and interests. Send hyper-relevant campaigns.

13. **[Voice of Customer Miner](skills/insights/voice-of-customer-miner.md)** → Mine product reviews and Reddit discussions. Find messaging angles and objection themes.

14. **[Pricing Page Optimizer](skills/cro/pricing-page-optimizer.md)** → Optimize your pricing page for conversions. Test tier names, benefits, social proof, and CTAs.

15. **[Retention Analysis Framework](skills/growth/retention-analysis-framework.md)** → Measure and improve repeat purchase rates. Identify which customers churn and why.

### Expected Outcomes

- **Conversion rate increases 20-40%** through landing page and checkout optimization
- **AOV grows 15-25%** via cross-sell and upsell email sequences
- **Customer retention improves 25-35%** with better post-purchase engagement
- **Email generates 20-30% of total revenue** as you segment and automate
- **CAC drops 20-30%** by eliminating underperforming paid channels and scaling winners
- **Seasonal revenue predictability** — you know how to capitalize on Black Friday, holidays, etc.

### Quick Start

1. **Week 1:** Build a customer persona. Export your top 50 customers from Shopify. What do they have in common?
2. **Week 2:** Audit your top 3 product pages with Landing Page Optimizer. Fix the lowest-scoring sections.
3. **Week 3:** Analyze your checkout funnel. Where do customers drop off? Run Checkout Funnel Optimizer.
4. **Week 4:** Design a 3-email post-purchase sequence. Test send to yourself.

Add email segmentation and retargeting in weeks 5-6.

## Tips for Success

**Invest in data quality first.** Bad data in → bad output out. Spend time cleaning and organizing your export before using a skill.

**Share outputs with your team.** Each skill produces artifacts (dashboards, briefs, sequences) that your team needs to see and feedback on. Make this collaborative.

**A/B test recommendations.** Skills give you recommendations, not guarantees. Test them on a small audience first (1% of email list, one landing page variant, etc.).

**Iterate, don't iterate once.** The pack gives you a starting point. After 4 weeks, revisit your personas, lead scoring model, and campaigns. Data changes; so should your strategy.

**Build feedback loops.** After implementing a skill, gather feedback from sales, customer success, or your team. Their insights inform the next iteration.
