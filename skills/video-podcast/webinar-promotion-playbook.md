# Webinar Promotion Playbook

Plans and executes multi-channel webinar promotion campaigns optimized for registration, attendance, and post-event engagement.

## Granularity Check

> **Time**: ~5 min data prep → ~10 min Claude session → ~30-60 min producing / uploading. If implementing full campaign, add 2-3 weeks lead time. Input is webinar details and audience data. Output is Markdown promotion plan with timeline, channel strategy, and email sequences.

## Process

### Step 1: Promotion Timeline

| Timeframe | Action | Channel | Goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| 4 weeks out | Announce webinar, open registration | Email, social, website | Build awareness |
| 3 weeks out | Speaker spotlight, topic teaser | Social, blog, partner outreach | Drive early registrations |
| 2 weeks out | Mid-campaign push, social proof | Email, paid ads, retargeting | Accelerate registrations |
| 1 week out | Urgency push, last chance | Email, social, countdown | Final registration push |
| 3 days out | Reminder with preview content | Email to registrants | Confirm attendance |
| Day of | Morning reminder + join link | Email, SMS (if opted in) | Maximize attendance |
| Day after | Recording + follow-up | Email to all (attended + no-show) | Post-event engagement |
| Week after | Content repurposing | Blog, social, email | Extend value |

### Step 2: Registration Page Optimization

| Element | Best Practice | Impact |
|---|---|---|
| Headline | Benefit-focused, not topic-focused | +20-30% conversion |
| Speaker bio | Credibility + photo | +15% trust |
| Bullet points | 3-5 specific takeaways | +10-20% conversion |
| Form fields | Name + email only (minimum) | Each additional field reduces conversion 5-10% |
| Social proof | Registrant count or past attendee quotes | +10-15% conversion |
| Urgency | Limited spots or countdown timer | +5-15% conversion |
| CTA button | Action-oriented ("Save My Spot") | +5-10% vs. generic "Register" |

### Step 3: Email Promotion Sequence

| Email | Timing | Subject Angle | Content |
|---|---|---|---|
| Announcement | 4 weeks out | Topic hook + value promise | What you'll learn, why it matters |
| Speaker spotlight | 3 weeks out | Speaker credibility | Speaker background, unique perspective |
| Social proof | 2 weeks out | "X people already registered" | Registrant count + testimonial |
| Urgency | 1 week out | "Spots filling up" / deadline | Limited availability + recap benefits |
| Final push | 3 days out | "Last chance to register" | Summary + strong CTA |
| Reminder 1 | 1 day before | "Tomorrow: [topic]" | Calendar add + prep materials |
| Reminder 2 | 1 hour before | "Starting soon — join now" | Direct join link |
| No-show follow-up | Day after | "We missed you — here's the recording" | Recording link + key takeaways |
| Attendee follow-up | Day after | "Thanks for joining + next steps" | Recording + resources + CTA |

### Step 4: Channel-Specific Tactics

| Channel | Tactic | Timing | Expected Impact |
|---|---|---|---|
| Email | Segmented sends to relevant lists | 4-5 sends over 4 weeks | 60-70% of registrations |
| LinkedIn | Organic posts + speaker shares + event page | 3x/week for 4 weeks | 10-20% of registrations |
| Twitter/X | Thread on topic + speaker engagement | 2x/week for 4 weeks | 5-10% of registrations |
| Paid social | LinkedIn/Meta lead gen ads | 2-3 weeks before | 15-25% of registrations (if budget) |
| Website | Banner, blog post, popup | Ongoing until event | 5-10% of registrations |
| Partners | Co-promotion emails + social shares | 3-2 weeks before | 10-30% of registrations |
| Community | Slack, Discord, forum posts | 2 weeks before | 5-10% of registrations |

### Step 5: Attendance Rate Optimization

| Tactic | Expected Lift | Effort |
|---|---|---|
| Calendar invite on registration | +10-15% attendance | Low |
| Pre-event content (worksheet, checklist) | +5-10% attendance | Medium |
| SMS reminder 1 hour before | +8-12% attendance | Low |
| Incentive for live attendance (bonus content) | +10-15% attendance | Medium |
| Shorter webinar (30-45 min vs. 60+) | +5-10% attendance | Low |
| Interactive elements (polls, Q&A, chat) | +5% attendance (word of mouth) | Low |
| Optimal timing (Tue-Thu, 11am-2pm local) | +5-10% attendance | Low |

### Step 6: Post-Webinar Conversion

| Segment | Follow-Up | Timing | CTA |
|---|---|---|---|
| Attended + engaged | Personal follow-up + offer | Within 24 hours | Demo/trial/consultation |
| Attended + passive | Recording + resources | Within 24 hours | Content download |
| Registered + no-show | Recording + key moments | Within 24 hours | Watch recording |
| Engaged non-buyer | Nurture sequence | Days 2-14 | Related content + soft CTA |

### Confidence & Sample Size

> **Confidence Note**: Average webinar registration-to-attendance rate is 35-45%. Email drives 60-70% of webinar registrations for most B2B companies. The biggest attendance driver is timing — Tuesday through Thursday between 11am-2pm in the target timezone consistently outperform other slots. Registration pages with 3 or fewer form fields convert 25-40% of visitors vs. 10-15% for longer forms. Promotional emails sent 2-4 weeks before the event drive the most registrations — campaigns shorter than 2 weeks underperform significantly.

### ⚠️ Human Checkpoint
> Verify all speaker details, date/time across timezones, and registration links before launching promotion. Test the full registration flow (form → confirmation email → calendar invite → reminder) before sending any promotional emails.

## Platform Implementation Steps

### Email Platform Setup
1. Create registration form with minimal fields
2. Set up automated confirmation email with calendar invite
3. Build reminder sequence (1 day before, 1 hour before)
4. Create post-event segments (attended, no-show, engaged)
5. Schedule follow-up emails for each segment

### Webinar Platform Setup
1. Configure registration page with tracking parameters (UTM codes)
2. Set up automated reminders within webinar platform
3. Prepare polls, Q&A, and interactive elements
4. Test recording settings and backup recording plan
5. Configure post-webinar redirect to offer page

### Social Media Setup
1. Create event-specific graphics (announcement, speaker spotlight, countdown)
2. Schedule organic posts across promotion timeline
3. Set up paid promotion campaigns with registration pixel
4. Brief speakers on social sharing schedule and sample copy
5. Create retargeting audience from page visitors who didn't register

## Failure Modes

| Failure | Cause | Recovery |
|---|---|---|
| Low registration | Weak topic hook or wrong audience | Rewrite headline focusing on outcome; narrow targeting |
| Low attendance | No reminder sequence or wrong timing | Add SMS reminders; test different day/time for next event |
| High drop-off during webinar | Too long, not interactive, poor audio/video | Shorten to 30-45 min; add polls every 5-7 min |
| No post-webinar conversion | No clear CTA or follow-up too slow | Send follow-up within 2 hours; include specific next step |

## How to Get Your Data

- **Past performance**: Webinar platform → analytics → registration and attendance rates
- **Email metrics**: Email platform → campaign reports → webinar-related sends
- **Registration sources**: UTM tracking → which channels drove registrations
- **No past data**: Describe your webinar topic and audience — Claude creates a promotion plan using industry benchmarks

## Example

**Input**: "Promotion plan for a webinar: 'AI-Powered Content Marketing in 2026.' Speaker: our VP of Marketing + guest from a content platform. Date: April 15, 2pm ET. Target: B2B content marketers. We have a 15K email list, 8K LinkedIn followers, no ad budget. Goal: 500 registrations."

**Output**: Timeline: Start promotion March 18 (4 weeks out). Channel mix: email (target 350 registrations — 70%), LinkedIn organic (target 100 — 20%), partner co-promotion with guest's company (target 50 — 10%). Email sequence: 5 promotional emails + 2 reminders. Email 1 (March 18): "How AI is changing content marketing in 2026" to full list. Email 2 (March 25): Speaker spotlight featuring guest expert to content-marketing-interest segment. Email 3 (April 1): "300 marketers already registered" social proof email. Email 4 (April 8): "Last week to register" urgency push. Email 5 (April 12): "Final seats" to non-registered contacts only. LinkedIn: 2 posts/week — announcement, speaker Q&A teaser, stat graphic, countdown. Ask guest speaker to share each post. Registration page: benefit headline "Learn the 5 AI Content Strategies Top B2B Teams Use in 2026", 3 takeaway bullets, name + email only, "Save My Spot" CTA, counter showing registrant count. Attendance target: 200-225 (40-45% of 500). Post-event: recording to all registrants within 4 hours, engaged attendees get personal outreach within 24 hours.
