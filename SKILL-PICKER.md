## Disambiguation Guide

Because some skill names overlap or sound similar, this section clarifies when to use each one.

### Personas & Segmentation: Four Related Skills

**Use `customer-persona-builder` (Growth)**
- When: Building detailed buyer archetypes with demographics, behaviors, motivations, pain points
- Output: 3-5 comprehensive personas to guide all marketing strategy
- Best for: Foundational audience research for new products or markets

**Use `audience-persona-builder` (Insights)**
- When: Creating audience profiles for messaging and content strategy
- Output: Persona summaries focusing on psychographics and messaging preferences
- Best for: Informing content, ad copy, and positioning

**Use `customer-segmentation-engine` (AI-Marketing)**
- When: Dividing your existing customer base into behavioral or demographic groups using AI/data
- Output: Dynamic segments based on purchase history, engagement, RFM scoring
- Best for: Email, CRM, and marketing automation targeting

**Use `segmentation-rule-builder` (CRM)**
- When: Setting up rules in your CRM or marketing automation platform
- Output: Rules-based lists (e.g., "high-value customers who haven't purchased in 90 days")
- Best for: Practical implementation in your tech stack

---

### Content Briefs: Three Related Skills

**Use `content-brief-generator` (SEO)**
- When: Creating briefs optimized for search intent and keyword targeting
- Output: SEO-focused brief with target keywords, search intent, suggested outline
- Best for: Blog posts and SEO content strategy

**Use `content-brief-writer` (Content)**
- When: Writing detailed, comprehensive briefs for writers and creators
- Output: Full specification including tone, style, length, examples, call-to-action
- Best for: All content types (blogs, landing pages, whitepapers, case studies)

**Use `campaign-brief-synthesizer` (Content)**
- When: Synthesizing research into a unified brief for a marketing campaign
- Output: Campaign brief covering positioning, messaging, audience, channels, success metrics
- Best for: Coordinating cross-functional campaigns (email, social, paid, etc.)

---

### Performance Analysis: Four Related Skills

**Use `ad-performance-analyzer` (Ads)**
- When: Analyzing paid ad campaigns (Google Ads, Meta, LinkedIn)
- Metrics: ROAS, CPC, CPM, conversion rate, impression share
- Best for: PPC campaign optimization

**Use `email-performance-analyzer` (Email)**
- When: Analyzing email campaign results
- Metrics: Open rate, click-through rate, conversion rate, unsubscribe rate
- Best for: Email campaign optimization and list health

**Use `social-engagement-analyzer` (Social)**
- When: Analyzing social media post and account performance
- Metrics: Engagement rate, reach, impressions, follower growth, sentiment
- Best for: Social strategy optimization

**Use `campaign-performance-benchmarker` (Analytics)**
- When: Comparing your campaign results against industry benchmarks
- Output: Performance gaps, industry comparisons, improvement opportunities
- Best for: Understanding competitive position and setting goals

---

### Pricing: Three Related Skills

**Use `pricing-page-optimizer` (CRO)**
- When: Improving the conversion rate of your pricing page
- Focus: Page layout, CTA clarity, form optimization, trust signals
- Best for: Increasing pricing page signups

**Use `pricing-research-framework` (Insights)**
- When: Determining optimal pricing strategy through market research
- Focus: Competitive pricing analysis, willingness to pay, pricing tiers
- Best for: Setting initial price or major price changes

**Use `pricing-page-optimizer` (Growth)**
- When: Using pricing as a growth lever (freemium to paid conversion, upselling)
- Focus: Pricing tier strategy, feature positioning, packaging
- Best for: GTM launches and pricing strategy

*Note: Both CRO and Growth have pricing skills. CRO focuses on conversion mechanics; Growth focuses on business strategy.*

### Audits & Compliance: Related Skills

**Use `technical-seo-auditor` (SEO)**
- When: Auditing crawlability, indexability, site architecture for search
- Output: Technical SEO issues and remediation roadmap
- Best for: SEO performance

**Use `on-page-seo-auditor` (SEO)**
- When: Auditing individual page elements (title, meta, headers, content)
- Output: On-page recommendations for each page
- Best for: Content optimization

**Use `email-compliance-checker` (Email)**
- When: Ensuring emails meet GDPR, CAN-SPAM, CASL, and ISP requirements
- Output: Compliance audit and fixes
- Best for: Email deliverability and legal compliance

**Use `marketing-compliance-pre-checker` (MarTech-Ops)**
- When: Pre-checking campaigns across channels for legal/brand compliance
- Output: Compliance checklist before launch
- Best for: Cross-channel campaign approval

**Use `crm-data-hygiene-auditor` (CRM)**
- When: Auditing CRM data quality, completeness, and consistency
- Output: Data quality report and remediation plan
- Best for: CRM health and segmentation accuracy
